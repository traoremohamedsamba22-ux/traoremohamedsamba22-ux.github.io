Statistics · Python
Real Estate Market Statistical Analysis

Full statistical analysis of the Ames Housing Dataset, comparing sale price distributions between two neighborhoods (North Ames vs College Creek) using rigorous statistical methods.

Descriptive statistics with Pandas (mean, std, median, min/max)
Distribution visualization: overlaid histograms & CDF curves
Kolmogorov-Smirnov test to compare two distributions
Shapiro-Wilk normality test + QQ-plots
Surface area (1stFlrSF) analysis across neighborhoods
Python Pandas SciPy Matplotlib Jupyter
📊 Result: Statistical confirmation (p < 0.05) that North Ames and College Creek have significantly different price and surface distributions — validating non-parametric test usage.
